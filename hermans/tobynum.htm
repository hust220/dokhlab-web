  • From Chapel Hill Bypass take 15-501 South to Pittsboro.

  • Off Bynum Road, turn left onto Bynum Hill. This is very soon after the Bynum Country Store (also on the left).
  • Bynum Hill turns sharply to the right by the water tower.
    Pass the first side street on the left (Bynum Church Road).
    Our house is at the end of the first driveway on the left after the corner.
