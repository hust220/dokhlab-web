• RNA Structure Prediction In a paper published today in the journal Nature Methods, Dokholyan group in collaboration with Weeks lab demonstrates a simple, cost-effective technique for three-dimensional RNA structure prediction that will help scientists understand the structure-function relationship of RNA molecules that dictate almost every aspect of human cell life. When cell behavior goes wrong, diseases – including cancer and metabolic disorders – can be the result.
